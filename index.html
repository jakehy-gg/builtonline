Local SEO — Explained Simply

When someone searches for what you do —
your business needs to come up first.

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation — but don't worry about the name. In plain terms, it just means making sure your business shows up near the top when someone types what you do into Google.

Think about it this way: if someone in your area types the name of your service into Google, three or four businesses appear at the top. Whoever those businesses are gets the phone call. If your name isn't there, that customer goes to a competitor — and they never even knew you existed.

That's what SEO fixes. We set your website up properly so Google shows you first.

1
People are already searching

Customers in your area are already searching for what you do, every single day. They just need to find you when they do.

2
The top results get almost all the calls

The first result on Google gets around ten times more clicks than the fifth. Position matters enormously — being on page two is almost the same as not being there at all.

3
It keeps working month after month

Unlike a paid advert that stops the moment you stop paying, good SEO builds over time and keeps generating enquiries without an ongoing cost per click.

Social Media — What It Actually Does

It's not about going viral. It's about being remembered.

When someone in your area needs a local contractor — they call the business they already know about. Social media is how you become that business, before they ever need you.

Awareness — People Learn You Exist

Someone in your area scrolls past a short video of your team completing a job. They've never heard of you. Now they have. That costs you nothing extra once the video is posted — it keeps being seen for months.

Most trades businesses rely entirely on word of mouth. Social media is word of mouth at scale — it reaches 10,000 people instead of 10, and it does it passively while you're getting on with the job.

Trust — People See Your Work Before They Call

When someone needs a contractor, they're already nervous about calling a stranger. If they've watched six videos of your team doing excellent work, that worry is gone. They call you confident and ready to book.

This is why video content is so powerful. It's not just advertising — it's proof. People can see the quality of your work before they ever pick up the phone.

Enquiries — Viewers Become Customers

Views turn into followers. Followers turn into enquiries. Not all at once — but steadily, month on month. 3 videos a week adds up to over 150 pieces of content in your first year, each one working for you indefinitely.

You don't need every video to go viral. You need consistent presence, so that when someone needs what you do, your name is already in their head.

Here's a real example of how this plays out.

It's September. A homeowner in your area scrolls past a video of your team finishing a job — clean work, professional finish, done on time. They're not ready to book yet. They just stop because the quality looks impressive. They follow you.

Two months later they're finally ready to get the work done. They don't Google it. They call you — because they've watched your videos for months and they already know the standard of your work.

Same story works for commercial jobs. A site manager or developer has watched six clips of your team working efficiently with no shortcuts. When a contract comes up, they call you — not a stranger from a directory.

That's not luck. That's what consistent content does. We build that for you.
